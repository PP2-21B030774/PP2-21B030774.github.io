Ninja-Pirate-Mink-Samurai Alliance
About Alliance:
"The Ninja-Pirato-Minko-Samurai Alliance" is an alliance formed between the Straw Hat Pirates, the Pirates of the Heart, the Kozuki family and the Minkami in order to overthrow one of them — Kaido.

Members:
Having set a goal to gather at least 5,000 people in its army, initially the alliance managed to gather only about 4,200 people. Shortly before the raid on Onigashima, Denjiro brought with him 1,200 people from the Flower Capital, which exceeded Kin'emon's initial expectations. The alliance consists of Pirates, Samurai and Minks.

History:
After the death of Kozuki Oden, his son Kozuki Momonosuke and some of his vassals were sent 20 years into the future, to the present, and began recruiting allies to avenge Kaido and Orochi for the death of their lord. After several incidents, Kin'emon befriended the Straw Hat Pirates, who formed an alliance with the Pirates of the Heart to jointly defeat Kaido. Traveling with the pirates for a while, Kin'emon began to appreciate their strength very much. After the alliance of Straw Hat Pirates and Heart Pirates defeated Caesar Klaun and Quixote Doflamingo, who used their connections in the criminal underground to cooperate with Kaido, the group headed to the Dzo to reunite with the rest of their allies.
