Straw Hat Grand Fleet
About Fleet:
"Straw Hat Grand Fleet" is a pirate fleet formed by seven organizations, under the command of Monky D. Luffy and the Straw Hat Pirates. In total, there are at least 5,640 members from these seven divisions, not counting the Straw Hat Pirates.
Foundaiton History:
After the events at the Dress Rose, the captains of each team asked Luffy if he could drink sake with them and become their "father", accepting everyone into his subordination. Luffy refused, citing his unwillingness to be some kind of "big shot". However, the captains decided to cheat and drink sake without Luffy's consent, pledging to come to the aid of his team whenever it is needed, while Luffy is not obliged to do the same. At this moment, the Great Straw Hat Fleet was formed.
